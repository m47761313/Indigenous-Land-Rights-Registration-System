# Indigenous Land Rights Registration System

A blockchain-based system for registering and protecting indigenous land rights, ensuring community consent, monitoring resource extraction, and preserving cultural heritage.

## Overview

This system consists of five interconnected smart contracts that work together to protect indigenous land rights:

1. **Traditional Land Recognition Contract** - Records and validates indigenous land claims
2. **Community Consent Management Contract** - Manages community decision-making processes
3. **Resource Extraction Monitoring Contract** - Tracks and regulates resource extraction activities
4. **Benefit Sharing Contract** - Ensures fair compensation distribution
5. **Cultural Heritage Protection Contract** - Protects sacred sites and traditional knowledge

## Key Features

### Land Recognition
- Register traditional land boundaries
- Document historical claims and customary rights
- Validate community ownership through consensus
- Immutable record of land titles

### Community Consent
- Democratic voting mechanisms for land use decisions
- Quorum requirements for valid decisions
- Transparent decision tracking
- Elder council integration

### Resource Monitoring
- Track mining, logging, and other extraction activities
- Environmental impact assessment requirements
- Compliance monitoring and reporting
- Automatic violation detection

### Benefit Sharing
- Fair compensation calculation and distribution
- Community fund management
- Transparent payment tracking
- Automatic royalty distribution

### Cultural Protection
- Sacred site registration and protection
- Traditional knowledge preservation
- Access control for sensitive areas
- Cultural impact assessments

## Contract Architecture

Each contract operates independently while maintaining data consistency through standardized interfaces. The system ensures:

- **Transparency**: All transactions are publicly verifiable
- **Immutability**: Land records cannot be altered without proper authorization
- **Community Control**: Indigenous communities maintain sovereignty over their lands
- **Fair Compensation**: Automated benefit sharing ensures equitable distribution

## Usage

### Registering Land Claims

1. Community submits land claim with boundaries and documentation
2. Elder council reviews and validates the claim
3. Community votes on acceptance
4. Land title is recorded on blockchain

### Managing Resource Extraction

1. External party requests extraction permits
2. Community consent process is initiated
3. Environmental and cultural impact assessments
4. Benefit sharing agreements established
5. Monitoring and compliance tracking begins

### Protecting Cultural Sites

1. Register sacred sites and sensitive areas
2. Set access permissions and restrictions
3. Monitor for unauthorized access
4. Enforce protection measures

## Security Considerations

- Multi-signature requirements for critical operations
- Time-locked decisions for major changes
- Community consensus mechanisms
- Audit trails for all transactions
